### Managed Funds System

**2018**

System of granting facilities from the place of managed funds, this system includes a user interface: web application (card of applicants and system experts). <br />
In this system, all processes are implemented in the BPMN2 standard language.

#### Information

-   **Applicant**: Ports and Maritime Organization of the Islamic Republic of Iran
-   **Employer**: [TIS Company](https://tis-co.ir/)
